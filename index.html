ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Wiktoria and I am an Illustrator and a UI designer originally from Poland, based in Belfast, Northern Ireland. I am very passionate and enthusiastic about my work. Always looking for new inspiration and fresh ideas. I love minimalism and simplicity in my designs. I love working with colour and creating fun and exciting designs. I never limit myself to my computer, I love working on paper using pens, markers, pencils and paintbrushes just as much as using my digital tools. Love for what I do is a key to my designs, and hopefully you can see this in my work.
